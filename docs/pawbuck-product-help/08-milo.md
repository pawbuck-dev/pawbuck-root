# Milo — in-app assistant

## Opening Milo

- **Bottom bar:** tap the **Milo** dog avatar in the circle **next to** the four main nav icons (Home, Records, Messages, Profile).
- **Home:** use the **Talk to Milo** (or similar) lead card near the top of the feed.
- **Health Records hub:** use the **floating plus-style** control; it opens Milo with **health records** context for that pet.
- **Pet Journal:** **Check in with Milo** runs a structured **AI journal interview** (counts against AI journal cap on Free).

The chat opens as a **modal** over your current screen (general Milo) or a full **journal chat** screen. Many shortcuts still require the correct pet to be selected in the app.

## What Milo can do

- Summarize vaccinations, medications, labs, exams, journal notes, and document vault items **for the selected pet** when you have access.
- Answer **how-to** questions about PawBuck features, plans, pet health workflows, and product help (RAG from PawBuck documentation).
- Give **brief, non-diagnostic** general pet-care tips (for example calming during loud noises, common barking triggers, or feeding discussion points)—always with a veterinarian disclaimer. Milo does **not** diagnose disease or prescribe doses.
- Help with **document uploads** in flows that send files through Milo-powered classification (for example generic **Add document** on the Health Records hub).

## What Milo cannot do

Milo does not diagnose disease or prescribe doses. For emergencies, contact a veterinarian immediately. Milo is not a substitute for professional veterinary care. Milo cannot change your App Store subscription—you must use **Profile → Subscription** or the in-app paywall.

## Plans and limits (Milo-specific)

| Feature | Free | Individual / Family |
|---------|------|---------------------|
| General Milo chat (modal) | **3 conversations lifetime** | Unlimited |
| AI journal interview ("Check in with Milo") | **2 sessions lifetime** | Unlimited |
| Manual journal notes (you type) | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Symptom decision trees (journal v1.5) | — | ✓ when enabled |
| Uses pet health data in answers | ✓ within access | ✓ |

After the **3rd** general Milo conversation on Free, the next message shows a paywall. After the **2nd completed** AI journal session on Free, starting a **new** AI interview shows a paywall. Continuing an in-progress journal session does not consume an extra slot until you complete it.

## Subscription or paywall

Upgrade prompts appear when you hit a limit or tap a **Family**- or **Individual**-only feature. Common sources:

- Milo chat cap (`Keep talking to Milo`)
- AI journal cap
- Document upload cap (10 on Free)
- Vet prep **brief teaser** vs full brief
- Pet Passport PDF
- **Manage Access** (Family)
- Second pet (Family)

Open **Profile → Subscription** to compare **Individual** vs **Family**, restore purchases, or see **Founding Member** offers when available. Billing is through Apple or Google.

For the full feature matrix, see the help article **Subscription plans — Free, Individual, and Family**.
