# Proactive care reminders

## What they are

PawBuck surfaces **care nudges** when vaccines, medications, documents, or journal patterns need attention. Rules are deterministic — based on your pet's health records, not guesses from unverified uploads.

## Where you see them

- **Home** — Up to three **Today's care reminders** with snooze (7 days) via the ✕ button.
- **Health record** — Vaccination and medication tabs reflect the same underlying rules.
- **Push** — A **daily digest** may summarize overdue vaccines, missing core vaccines, document expiry, or senior wellness tips (when enabled).

Tap any reminder to open the relevant health record screen.

## Push vs on-device reminders

| Type | Where to control |
|------|------------------|
| Daily journal prompt | **Profile → Reminders** (local notification on this device) |
| Vaccine care digest | **Profile → Reminders → Vaccine care reminders** |
| Insurance & travel expiry | **Profile → Reminders** (Individual plan) |
| Vet visit 24h / 1h | **Profile → Reminders** (Individual plan) |
| Medication due today | Local notifications scheduled from the health record |

## Snooze and dismiss

On Home, tap ✕ on a care reminder to **snooze it for 7 days** for that pet and reminder type. Snoozed items stay hidden until the snooze date passes.

## Milo and proactive copy

Some wellness tips (for example senior mobility hints after journal notes) may use **Milo** for a short friendly sentence. Clinical overdue and missing-vaccine reminders use fixed, vet-reviewable templates — not AI diagnosis.

## Limitations

- Reminders only fire from **verified clinical rows** (for example vaccinations with an administered date synced from a certificate).
- A due-only line on a PDF without a synced vaccination row does **not** create an overdue nudge.
- Push delivery respects a daily cap so you are not flooded with alerts.

For notification permissions, see **Profile → Settings → Push permissions**.
