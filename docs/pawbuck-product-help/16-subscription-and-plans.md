# Subscription plans — Free, Individual, and Family

PawBuck v1.5 uses three plans. Prices are in **USD**; exact billing is handled by the **App Store** or **Google Play** through in-app purchase (RevenueCat).

## Plan summary

| Plan | Monthly | Annual | Lifetime (Founding) | Pets | Family members |
|------|---------|--------|---------------------|------|----------------|
| **Free** | $0 | — | — | 1 | — |
| **Individual** | $5.99 | $49.99/year | $34.99 one-time (Founding) | 1 | — |
| **Family** | $9.99 | $79.99/year | $54.99 one-time (Founding) | Unlimited | Up to 5 |

## What stays free on every plan

These work on **Free** with no upgrade:

- One pet profile and manual health logging
- **Pet Journal** (manual entries) and **Pawthon** streak / weekly challenge / GPS walks
- **Book a vet visit** flow (when enabled in your app version)
- **Pet ownership transfer**
- Bottom navigation, Messages inbox, and viewing records you already have

## Free plan limits (lifetime counters)

On **Free**, these caps apply to your **account** (not per month):

| Limit | Free allowance | Individual / Family |
|-------|----------------|---------------------|
| Milo AI chat conversations | **3 lifetime** | Unlimited |
| Milo **AI journal** check-ins (structured interview) | **2 lifetime** | Unlimited |
| Health **document uploads** (see counting rules below) | **10 total** | Unlimited |
| Pet profiles | **1** | Individual: 1 · Family: unlimited |
| Family sharing (**Manage Access**) | Not available | Family only (up to 5 members) |

**Manual journal entries** (typing a note yourself) are **not** counted against the AI journal cap—only Milo-guided **AI journal interviews** count.

## Individual plan — what you unlock

Upgrade to **Individual** when you need:

- **Unlimited Milo** conversations and AI journal check-ins
- **Unlimited** vet document uploads (photos, PDFs, health tab uploads)
- **Full vet prep briefs** (Free shows a teaser on Health Briefing)
- **Pet email parsing** — health attachments sent to your pet's **@pawbuck.app** address are automatically classified and imported (Free accounts receive the email but health OCR/import requires Individual)
- **Pet Passport PDF** export from pet profile (**Download Pet Passport**)
- **Health alerts & reminders** tied to premium entitlements (insurance/travel expiry pushes where configured)
- **Symptom decision trees** in Milo journal mode (when enabled)
- Second pet on the same account → app prompts **Family** (Individual allows only one pet)

## Family plan — everything in Individual plus

- **Unlimited pets** on one account
- **Family sharing** — invite up to **5** household members via **Profile → Manage Access**
- **Multi-pet dashboard** and per-pet email addresses for each pet
- **Three-tier permissions** (View only, Contributor, Admin) for invited members

## How document uploads are counted (Free cap)

The **10 document** limit counts **across your account**, including:

- Files in **Documents & ID** on the Health Records hub
- Vaccination, medication, lab, and exam rows that store an uploaded **document URL**

When you reach **10**, the next upload shows an upgrade prompt. Upgrade to **Individual** for unlimited uploads.

## When you see an upgrade prompt

| Moment | What happens on Free | Suggested plan |
|--------|----------------------|----------------|
| 4th Milo chat message (new conversation) | Paywall — "Keep talking to Milo" | Individual |
| 3rd AI journal interview start | Paywall — AI journal cap | Individual |
| 11th document upload | Paywall — document cap | Individual |
| 10+ day Pawthon streak | Upgrade banner on Home | Individual |
| Health Briefing / vet prep | Teaser only; full brief locked | Individual |
| Pet Passport PDF | Locked on pet profile | Individual |
| Email with health attachments (Free) | Push notification to upgrade; auto-import skipped | Individual |
| Add 2nd pet | Family paywall | Family |
| **Manage Access** / family invites | Family paywall | Family |
| 6th family member | Blocked at cap | Family (max 5 members) |

## Managing your subscription

Open **Profile** → **Subscription** (or upgrade rows shown for your plan). You can:

- See your current plan (**Free**, **Individual**, or **Family**)
- Open the in-app paywall to compare plans
- **Restore purchases** after reinstalling (uses App Store / Play billing)

Billing, refunds, and cancellation follow **Apple** or **Google** subscription rules. PawBuck does not charge your card directly outside those stores.

## Founding Member (lifetime)

During launch, a limited **Founding Member** offer may appear:

- **Individual lifetime:** $34.99 one-time (non-renewing)
- **Family lifetime:** $54.99 one-time (non-renewing)
- **Cap:** 500 total founding purchases across both SKUs — when sold out, lifetime options hide
- **Badge:** **Founding Member** on Profile for purchasers

Founding purchases grant the same entitlements as paid Individual or Family, plus the badge.

## Legacy "Premium" or "Pro"

Older app versions or receipts may reference **Premium** or **Pawbuck Pro**. That maps to **Individual** in current builds.

## Milo and subscription questions

If Milo says you hit a limit, check **Profile** for your plan or tap **Upgrade** on the paywall. Milo cannot change your billing—use in-app purchase or store subscription settings to upgrade or restore.
