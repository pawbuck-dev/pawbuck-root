## Visual Style Guide

### Colors
- **Primary Background:** #0A0A0A (dark) or #FAFAFA (light)
- **Primary Accent:** #5FC4C0 (teal) or #0D9488 (darker teal)
- **Text:** #FFFFFF (white) or #1A1A1A (dark)
- **Secondary Text:** #9CA3AF (gray) or #737373 (light gray)

### Typography
- **Font Family:** Outfit (matching app)
- **Headings:** Bold, 36-48px
- **Body Text:** Regular, 18-24px
- **Overlays:** Semi-bold, 24-32px

### Effects
- **Transitions:** Smooth fades (0.3-0.5s)
- **Animations:** Subtle, professional
- **Highlights:** Teal glow on key elements
- **Shadows:** Soft, subtle depth

### Screen Recording Settings
- **Resolution:** 1080p (1920x1080)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps (60fps for smooth animations)
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Device:** iPhone 14 Pro or similar (for best quality)
